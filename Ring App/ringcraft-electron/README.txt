Ring Crafting Database - Desktop App
=====================================

FIRST-TIME SETUP (one time only):
1. Install Node.js from https://nodejs.org  (LTS version)
2. Double-click "Run Ring Crafting Database.bat"
   - It will automatically install dependencies the first time (~2 minutes)
   - The app will open in its own window

AFTER SETUP:
Just double-click "Run Ring Crafting Database.bat" to open the app.

YOUR DATA:
All data saves automatically to your browser's localStorage.
It persists between sessions — your rings, prices, and sales orders
are always restored when you reopen the app.

FEATURES:
- Database tab: manage ring recipes, ingredient costs, sale prices
- Full Recipe tab: see complete material breakdown for any ring
- Sales tab: build multi-ring orders, mark customer-supplied materials,
  get a full quote summary showing your cost and profit
